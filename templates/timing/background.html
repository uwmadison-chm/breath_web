The Study

We invite you to be aware of your breath. While pressing a key with each breath, you may be asked occasionally about your experience. This will help us understand how the mind works. Most sessions last 10-15 minutes, though you can choose to go longer or return for more sessions. If you become uncomfortable, you may stop at any time.

Our intention is to freely offer a safe, secure environment to investigate the mind, for visitors and for the field of science. If you have concerns about what you experience during the session please contact Dr. Richard Davidson (rjdavids@wisc.edu; 608-265-8189). If you have questions about your rights as a research participant, please contact the Education and Social and Behavioral Science Institutional Review Board ("Committee for the Protection of Human Subjects") at 608-263-2320.

No personal benefit is guaranteed from participating in this study.
